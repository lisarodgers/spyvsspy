# Spy vs Spy Computers

## Mission

Spy vs Spy Computers doesn't tell people what to think.

It shows them the evidence and explains why.

## Core Principles

- Privacy First
- Evidence Based
- Transparent
- Educational
- Secure by Design
- Self-hostable
- Open Architecture

## First Product

Abuse Triage Analyzer

A privacy-first platform for investigating suspicious email, headers, IP addresses, and digital abuse.

Users own their data.
Users understand the evidence.
